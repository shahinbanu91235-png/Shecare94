Hair Care Routine

Daily Hair Care Routine

Straight Hair

• Comb gently using wide-tooth comb
• Apply light serum if needed
• Avoid frequent touching

Wavy Hair

• Finger comb or wide-tooth comb
• Use light leave-in conditioner
• Avoid dry brushing

Curly Hair

• Finger detangle only
• Apply curl cream / leave-in conditioner
• Protect from dryness

Coily / Kinky Hair

• Daily moisturizing with oil or cream
• Gentle finger detangling
• Cover hair with scarf/bonnet

2 Days Once Hair Care Routine

Straight Hair

• Wash with mild shampoo
• Light conditioner on ends
• Air dry naturally

Wavy Hair

• Sulfate-free shampoo
• Moisturizing conditioner
• Scrunch gently

Curly Hair

• Moisturizing shampoo
• Rich conditioner
• Apply leave-in after wash

Coily / Kinky Hair

• Gentle wash if required
• Deep conditioner
• Seal moisture with oil

Weekly Hair Care Routine

Straight Hair

• Oil once or twice a week
• Smoothening hair mask
• Trim split ends if needed

Wavy Hair

• Oil once a week
• Hydrating hair mask
• Trim every 3 months

Curly Hair

• Oil twice a week
• Deep conditioning mask weekly
• Trim every 3–4 months

Coily / Kinky Hair

• Oil 2–3 times a week
• Intensive deep conditioning
• Trim every 4 months
